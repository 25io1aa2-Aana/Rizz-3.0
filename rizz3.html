SYSTEM ADVANTAGES
RIZZ 3.0 · ESP32 SURVEILLANCE PLATFORM · TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
01
High Performance & Cost-Effectiveness
The ESP32 dual-core 240MHz processor delivers powerful real-time processing at a fraction of traditional surveillance costs. Built-in WiFi 802.11 b/g/n and Bluetooth 4.2 eliminate the need for external networking hardware, reducing BOM costs by up to 60% versus comparable solutions.
02
Live Video Feed and Mobility
The OV2640 camera module on ESP32-CAM provides 2MP live MJPEG streaming accessible from any browser on the local network. The differential drive system enables 360° maneuverability, allowing remote inspection of confined, hazardous, or inaccessible areas in real time.
03
All-Day Operations (Night Vision & Infrared)
940nm IR LEDs provide covert illumination invisible to the human eye, enabling 24/7 surveillance without visible light signature. The ESP32-CAM's adjustable gain and exposure allow clear imaging in near-zero lux conditions up to 8–10 meters, making it effective in complete darkness.
04
Advanced Tracking and Connectivity
Integrated NEO-6M GPS module provides sub-3m accuracy for real-time path recording and replay. The system maintains a persistent TCP connection for control commands while streaming video over HTTP — supporting simultaneous operation via the dual-core architecture without latency tradeoffs.
05
Smart Features & Versatility
Beyond surveillance, the RIZZ 3.0 platform supports motion-triggered recording, autonomous path replay, OTA firmware updates, and expandable I²C sensors. The modular design supports ultrasonic obstacle avoidance, environmental sensing, and edge AI inference directly on the ESP32 for fully autonomous operation.
